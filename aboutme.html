About Me

My name is Jairo Arauz, I am a current BMCC student.
I am currently pursuing an Associate in Science (A.S.) degree.

Right after High School, I had the opportunity to start a career in media for major companies
such as Viacom/MTV Networks, Discovery and Vme Media. My resposibilities included
the scheduling of commercials and the live programming for a spanish network.

My schedule was always busy, it was hard to finish my Graphic Design studies.
I kept postponing my education, because I wanted to take advantage of every opportunity
that opened up for me. In 2015, I decided to change my career and look for a job
that would allow me to finish my education.

I always had a passion for Art. I enjoy the work of others drawing, photography
and digital designings. Such work inspire and motivate me to pursue my dream as a Graphic
and Web Designer. I can't wait to use my creativity and new skills to create attractive websites.
